Fudge Brownies

Indulge in rich chocolate brownies: melt chocolate with butter, mix in sugar, eggs, and vanilla, fold in flour and cocoa, bake until gooey. Enjoy every decadent bite!

Ingredients

  • 200g ground almonds
  • 200g butter
  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 100g milk chocolate
  • 150-200g sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 level teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 package vanilla sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Start by melting chocolate and butter in a heatproof bowl over simmering water. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius with both top and bottom heating. Grease a baking dish with butter.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine eggs, sugar, and vanilla sugar. Stir until smooth. Once the chocolate and butter have melted completely, add the melted chocolate to the egg mixture. Stir until smooth.
  3. Next, add flour, ground almonds, baking powder, and salt to the mixture. Stir until well combined.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes.
  5. Enjoy your delicious brownies!
