Homemade Pasta

Homemade pasta is a delightful culinary endeavor involving combining flour and eggs into a dough, which is then rolled out, shaped, and cooked to perfection, offering a fresh and flavorful alternative to store-bought varieties.

Ingredients

  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 table olive oil
  • 1-2 tablespoons water (if needed)

Steps

  1. On a clean work surface, mound the flour and create a well in the center.
  2. Crack the eggs into the well and add the salt.
  3. Using a fork, gradually incorporate the flour into the eggs until a dough starts to form.
  4. Once the dough comes together, knead it for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough feels too dry, you can add a little water, one tablespoon at a time.
  5. Once kneaded, shape the dough into a ball and wrap it in plastic wrap. Let it rest at room temperature for about 30 minutes to allow the gluten to relax.
  6. After resting, divide the dough into smaller portions. Flatten each portion with a rolling pin or pasta machine until it reaches your desired thickness. You can dust the dough with flour to prevent sticking.
  7. Cut the rolled-out dough into your desired pasta shape. You can make fettuccine, spaghetti, or any other shape you prefer.
  8. Once you've cut the pasta, you can cook it immediately in boiling salted water for 2-4 minutes, or until al dente.
  9. Drain the cooked pasta and serve it with your favorite sauce, such as marinara, Alfredo, or pesto.
  10. Enjoy your homemade pasta! It's simple, delicious, and perfect for any occasion
